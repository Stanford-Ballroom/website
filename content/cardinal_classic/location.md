## Competition Venue

Cardinal Classic will take place in the **basketball courts at Arrillaga Centers for Sports & Recreation (ACSR) Gym** on Stanford University campus.
<br>
The address is **341 Galvez St, Stanford, CA 94305** ([Google Maps](https://maps.app.goo.gl/jHQvcMFXJbcnY7NQ9?g_st=ic)).
![ACSR front](cardinal-classic/acsr-front.png)


Please enter directly onto the basketball courts from the entrance on Campus Dr.

![ACSR basketball courts entrance](cardinal-classic/acsr-entrance.jpeg)


## Parking

There is plenty of free parking available near ACSR on the weekends. We've circle some of the nearby spots below. Please make sure to always double check the posted sign: e.g. any C or visitor spot should be unenforced on the weekends (only enforced 6 am – 4 pm, M - F unless otherwise posted), and thus free to park on the day of the competition.

![parking near ACSR](cardinal-classic/acsr-nearby-parking.jpeg)
